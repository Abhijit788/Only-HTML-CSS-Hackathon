About Strike

Empowering learners worldwide with cutting-edge education technology and expert-led courses

Our Mission

At Strike, we believe that quality education should be accessible to everyone, everywhere. Our mission is to democratize learning by providing world-class courses taught by industry experts and delivered through innovative technology platforms.

We're committed to breaking down barriers to education and creating opportunities for personal and professional growth, regardless of your background or location.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a team of passionate educators and technologists, Strike emerged from a simple observation: traditional education wasn't keeping pace with the rapidly evolving digital world.

We started with a vision to create an online learning platform that would combine the best of traditional teaching methods with cutting-edge technology, making high-quality education accessible to learners across the globe.
